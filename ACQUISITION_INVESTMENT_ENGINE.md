**Date**: 2026-05-25
**Status**: Framework deployed — currently in monitoring/education mode

## Engine Overview

The Acquisition & Investment Engine scans for, evaluates, and scores external opportunities (acquisitions, investments, strategic partnerships) that could accelerate Wheeler's growth. At the current pre-revenue stage, it operates in **monitoring mode** — building opportunity awareness without active deal pursuit.

## Valuation Quick-Reference

### Micro-SaaS (<$100K ARR)
- Growing (>20% YoY): 3-5x ARR
- Stable (0-20% YoY): 2-3x ARR
- Declining: 1-2x ARR (or asset value)
- Distressed: <1x ARR or asset value

### Data Assets
- Unique/proprietary data: $0.10-1.00 per record (depending on value)
- Commonly available data: near $0
- Data pipeline/infrastructure: replacement cost

### Domain Names
- Exact match .com: $1K-$100K+
- Category-defining: $10K-$1M+
- Brand protection: registration cost
- Portfolio: 12-24x annual revenue

## Synergy Analysis Framework

For each target, quantify 5 synergy types:

```
REVENUE SYNERGY
├── Cross-sell Wheeler to target's customers: +$X/mo potential
├── Cross-sell target to Wheeler's customers: +$X/mo potential
├── Bundled offering premium pricing: +$X/mo potential
└── Total Revenue Synergy: +$X/mo

COST SYNERGY
├── Eliminate duplicate infrastructure: -$X/mo
├── Consolidate SaaS tools: -$X/mo
├── Shared AI/API costs: -$X/mo
└── Total Cost Synergy: -$X/mo

TECHNOLOGY SYNERGY
├── Accelerate Wheeler roadmap by X months
├── Add capability Wheeler would build anyway
└── Technology Value: $X (replacement cost)

DATA SYNERGY
├── Enrich Wheeler datasets (quality improvement)
├── Enable new data products
└── Data Value: $X (licensing equivalent)

TEAM SYNERGY
├── Add X engineers/specialists
├── Hiring cost avoided: $X (recruiter fees + ramp time)
└── Team Value: $X
```
