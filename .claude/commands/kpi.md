# /kpi — Enterprise KPI Dashboard

Display the complete enterprise KPI dashboard with current values, benchmarks, and trends.

## Execution

Launch the `enterprise-kpi-intelligence` agent to produce the full KPI report:

1. Revenue KPIs: MRR, ARR, ARPU, Revenue Concentration
2. Growth KPIs: MoM Growth, YoY Growth, Net New MRR, SaaS Quick Ratio
3. Unit Economics: CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC, CAC Payback
4. Retention KPIs: Logo Churn, Gross MRR Churn, Net MRR Churn, NDR
5. Efficiency KPIs: Gross Margin, Operating Margin, Rule of 40, Burn Multiple, Magic Number
6. AI Efficiency KPIs: AI Cost per $ Revenue, Cost per Task, Automation Rate

Each KPI displayed with:
- Current value vs. prior period
- Benchmark comparison (Green/Yellow/Red)
- Trend indicator (improving/stable/deteriorating)

## Output
- Composite KPI Health Score (0-100)
- Color-coded KPI grid (Green/Yellow/Red)
- KPIs requiring attention
- Correlation insights (leading indicators flagging issues)
