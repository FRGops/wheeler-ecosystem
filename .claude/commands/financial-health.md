# /financial-health — Full Ecosystem Financial Health Audit

Run a complete financial health check across all domains. Unlike /cfo (executive summary), this produces detailed per-agent reports.

## Execution (launch in parallel)

Invoke these agents simultaneously for a full financial picture:

1. **infrastructure-cost** — per-service cost allocation, right-sizing opportunities
2. **ai-token-cost** — per-model, per-key AI spend breakdown
3. **ai-spending-governance** — budget status, anomaly detection, guardrail status
4. **vendor-optimization** — SaaS subscription audit, upcoming renewals
5. **resource-allocation** — cost-per-workload, capacity utilization scores
6. **scaling-cost-forecast** — 30/90/180-day cost projections
7. **treasury-intelligence** — cash position, liquidity forecast
8. **cashflow-forecasting** — 13-week cash flow projection
9. **financial-governance** — policy compliance, agent authority audit

## Output
Consolidate into a Financial Health Report:
- Overall Financial Health Score
- Per-domain breakdown (Infrastructure, AI, Vendor, Treasury, Governance)
- Active alerts by severity
- Optimization opportunities ranked by ROI
- Top 5 recommended actions
